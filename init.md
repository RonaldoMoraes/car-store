We're about to start a company that sells websites and apps for car dealerships. Our goal is to provide high-quality, user-friendly digital solutions that help dealerships attract more customers and easily control their inventory. To achieve this, we will focus on the following key areas:

0. **IMPORTANT**: This project is intended to be a white label solution for car dealerships, meaning that the websites and apps we create will be fully customizable and branded to reflect the dealership's identity. This will allow dealerships to maintain their unique brand image while benefiting from our expertise in digital solutions. Also it's intended to be firstly used only by brazilian car dealerships, but we will be open to expand to other countries in the future.
1. **Custom Website Design**: We will create visually appealing and responsive websites with different pre-set templates tailored to the automotive industry. Each design will be optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring a seamless user experience for potential customers.
2. **Inventory Management Integration**: Our websites will feature integrated inventory management systems that allow dealerships to easily update and showcase their vehicle listings. This will include real-time updates, search filters, and detailed vehicle information to enhance the browsing experience for users.
3. **Inventory and store management**: We will provide dealerships with a comprehensive inventory and store management system that allows them to easily add, edit, and remove vehicle listings. This system will also include features such as pricing management, vehicle categorization, and inventory tracking to help dealerships efficiently manage their stock through a **native white-label app**
4. **Lead Generation Tools**: We will implement lead generation tools such as contact forms, live chat support, and call-to-action buttons to capture potential customer information and facilitate communication between dealerships and interested buyers. These tools will help dealerships convert website visitors into leads and ultimately increase sales.
5. **White-label B2B Native App for Dealerships**: In addition to websites, we will develop a native mobile app specifically designed for car dealerships. This app will allow dealerships to manage their inventory, store customers information, and access sales data on-the-go. Main features are to include the easiest ans smoothest way to add cars to inventory, managing their information.
6, **Tabela FIPE**: In Brazil, we will integrate the Tabela FIPE (a widely recognized vehicle price reference) into our platform, allowing dealerships to provide accurate pricing information for their vehicles. This feature will help build trust with potential buyers and ensure that dealerships are offering competitive prices in the market.

**Additional Info**:
The goal is to help car dealerships in Brazil to manage their inventories easily and effectively, while also providing them with a strong online presence to attract more customers. By offering a white-label solution, we aim to empower dealerships to maintain their brand identity while leveraging our expertise in digital solutions. Our focus on user-friendly design, inventory management, lead generation, and mobile app functionality will ensure that dealerships can operate efficiently and grow their business in the competitive automotive market.

The main collaborators we need is obviously a CEO, CTO, and a full engineering and Product team with UI/UX specialists and Native apps developers. That's the ful focus, we're aiming to have a full functional product ready for launch before marketing and sales effort begins, because I already have possible stores and dealerships interested in our solution. The idea is to have a strong product that can be easily customized and branded for each dealership, allowing them to quickly establish their online presence and manage their inventory effectively.
